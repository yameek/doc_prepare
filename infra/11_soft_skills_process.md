# 11. Soft-skills & Process

Technology is easy; people are hard. This is what makes you a Senior/Staff Engineer.

## Junior -> Mid: The Basics
**Goal:** Communicates clearly and follows the process.

### 1. Documentation
- **Concept:** Writing it down.
- **Key Skills:**
    - **README:** Explaining how to run the project.
    - **Tickets:** Writing clear bug reports with reproduction steps.

### 2. Incident Response
- **Concept:** Don't panic.
- **Key Skills:**
    - **Escalation:** Knowing when to ask for help.
    - **Communication:** Updating the status page or team channel.

---

## Mid -> Senior: Leadership & Culture
**Goal:** Improves the team's velocity and safety.

### 1. Everything as Code
- **Concept:** No manual changes.
- **Key Knowledge:**
    - **Merge Request Flow:** Reviewing code for quality and security.
    - **Peer Review:** Giving constructive feedback.

### 2. Incident Management
- **Concept:** Learning from failure.
- **Key Knowledge:**
    - **Blameless Post-Mortems:** Focusing on *process* failure, not *human* error.
    - **5 Whys:** Digging to the root cause.
    - **On-Call:** Managing hand-offs and preventing burnout.

### 3. Architecture Decision Records (ADRs)
- **Concept:** Documenting the "Why".
- **Key Knowledge:**
    - **Format:** Context, Decision, Consequences.
    - **History:** Creating a log of why we chose Kubernetes over Nomad.

---

## Senior -> Lead: Strategy & Influence
**Goal:** Sets the technical direction and mentors others.

### 1. Technical Communication
- **Concept:** Selling ideas.
- **Key Knowledge:**
    - **Diagrams:** C4 Model, Excalidraw. Visualizing complex systems.
    - **Tech Talks:** Sharing knowledge with the wider org.

### 2. Runbooks
- **Concept:** Automating operations.
- **Key Knowledge:**
    - **Executable Runbooks:** Jupyter notebooks or scripts that guide an operator through a fix.
    - **Maintenance:** Keeping docs up to date.

### 3. Mentorship
- **Concept:** Multiplying yourself.
- **Key Knowledge:**
    - **Sponsorship:** Advocating for juniors.
    - **Code Review:** Using reviews as a teaching moment, not just gatekeeping.
